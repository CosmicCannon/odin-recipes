Spinach Artichoke Dip

This slow cooker spinach-artichoke dip is easy to make and delicious!

  • Prep: 5 mins
  • Cook: 2 hrs
  • Total: 2 hr 5 mins
  • Servings: 6
  • Yield: 6 servings

    • Nutritional Info

    Ingredients

    • 1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
    • 1 (9 ounce) package frozen spinach
    • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
    • ¾ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
    • ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
    • ¼ cup milk
    • salt and ground black pepper to taste

    Directions

    1. Step - Combine artichokes, spinach, cream cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, milk, salt, and pepper in the bottom of a slow cooker.
    2. Step - Cover and cook on High, stirring occasionally to combine, for 2 hours.
