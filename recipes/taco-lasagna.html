Taco Lasagna

This is a fun recipe and I guarantee every one will love it. So try it and enjoy!

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 45
  • Addtional: 10
  • Total: 1 hr 15 mins
  • Servings: 12
  • Yield: 1-9x13 inch pan

    • Nutritional Info

    Ingredients

    • 2 pounds lean ground beef
    • 2 (1.25) packages taco seasoning mix
    • 4 cloves garlic, minced
    • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
    • 1 tablespoon chili powder
    • ½ cup water
    • 18 (6 inch) corn tortillas
    • 1 (24 ounce) jar salsa
    • 1 cup sliced green onion
    • 1 (16 ounce))container sour cream
    • 1½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese
    • 1½ cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese

    Directions

    1. Step - Place ground beef in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain, then season with taco seasoning, garlic, cayenne pepper, chili powder and water. Simmer for 10 minutes.
    2. Step - Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    3. Step - Place 6 tortillas into the prepared baking dish. Spread 1/3 of the salsa on top of the tortillas. Spread 1/2 of the meat mixture evenly over the salsa. Sprinkle with 1/2 of the green onions. Drop 1/2 of the sour cream randomly over the green onions. Top with 1/2 cup Cheddar and 1/2 cup Monterey Jack cheese. Repeat layers. Top with 6 tortillas, spread with remaining salsa, and sprinkle with remaining cheese.
    4. Step - Bake in a preheated oven for 30 to 45 minutes or until cheeses are melted.
